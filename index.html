Naman Taggar

I am an under-graduate student of Mathematics at Sri Guru Tegh Bahadur (SGTB) Khalsa College, University of Delhi, with particular interests lying in algebra. I am interested in many other things including design, typesetting, music and photography.

Projects.  The things I have worked on can be found here.

Highlights.  I contributed as part of Organising Committee to the International Conference on Mathematical Sciences and its Applications (ICMSA 2024) organised by Department of Mathematics, Sri Guru Tegh Bahadur (SGTB) Khalsa College, University of Delhi during 15–17 October, 2024. Over 140 delegates from India and abroad joined the conference. I also delivered a presentation entitled “Finite Group Theory in Mathematica” where I demonstrated the use of a Mathematica package I developed.

I have been editing the official magazine of SGTB Khalsa College since 2023. The annual document is over 200 pages in length and over 500 hard-copies are printed every year. Furthermore, I have also contributed to the re-curation of the college's official website by providing content and graphics. I performed an analysis of marks and attendance in the college using R, and talked about it in this blog post.

I have joined numerous workshops including 2 national workshops, on topics varying from programming with Python to numerical optimisation.

Skills & Languages.  I speak English and Hindi. I know Mathematica, Python (base, numpy, pandas), R (base, dyplr, ggplot2), and a little bit of Javascript. I am well-experienced with LaTeX, and familiar with Inkscape (vector graphics editing), HTML, CSS, and Markdown.
